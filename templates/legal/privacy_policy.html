Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Lerzo ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and maintained by Macho Dev LLP, a registered company. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational management software.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, institution details
  • Student Data: Enrollment information, academic records, payment details
  • Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Process transactions and send receipts
  • Notify you about changes to our Service
  • Monitor usage and improve our Service
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security

3. Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your data, including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Regular security audits
  • Role-based access controls
  • Secure payment processing through Razorpay

All user data is safe in our hands. We take full responsibility for safeguarding your information.

4. Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies for:

  • Payment processing (Razorpay)
  • Cloud hosting services
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

These third parties are authorized only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Until a deletion request is made
  • Financial records: 7 years for tax compliance
  • Student records: As required by educational regulations

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@lerzo.in.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for educational institutions and not directly to children under 13. We comply with:

  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
  • FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) where applicable

8. Changes to This Policy

We may change our Privacy Policy whenever we deem necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed.
